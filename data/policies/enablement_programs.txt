ECONOMIC ENABLEMENT PROGRAMS CATALOG
=====================================

PROGRAM 1: DIGITAL SKILLS ACCELERATOR
Eligibility: Unemployed or underemployed individuals aged 18-55
Duration: 3 months
Description: Comprehensive digital literacy program covering computer basics,
internet usage, Microsoft Office suite, email communication, and introduction
to digital tools used in modern workplaces.
Outcome: Digital Skills Certificate recognized by government and private employers.
Recommended For: Applicants with limited technology skills, career changers.

PROGRAM 2: PROFESSIONAL CERTIFICATION FAST-TRACK
Eligibility: Individuals with relevant work experience seeking formal qualifications
Duration: 2-6 months depending on certification
Description: Subsidized professional certification programs in accounting (ACCA),
project management (PMP), IT (CompTIA, Cisco), healthcare, and hospitality.
Outcome: Internationally recognized professional certification.
Recommended For: Experienced workers lacking formal qualifications.

PROGRAM 3: ENTREPRENEURSHIP LAUNCHPAD
Eligibility: Self-employed or aspiring entrepreneurs
Duration: 6 months
Description: Business plan development, financial management, marketing strategy,
legal compliance, and access to micro-finance. Includes mentorship from
established business owners.
Outcome: Business launch support and seed funding eligibility.
Recommended For: Self-employed applicants, those with business ideas.

PROGRAM 4: VOCATIONAL TRAINING - CONSTRUCTION & TRADES
Eligibility: Male applicants aged 18-50 with physical fitness certification
Duration: 3-6 months
Description: Hands-on training in electrical work, plumbing, welding, carpentry,
and HVAC maintenance. Includes safety certifications.
Outcome: Trade certification and direct job placement with partner companies.
Recommended For: Unemployed males with limited education, career changers.

PROGRAM 5: HEALTHCARE ASSISTANT TRAINING
Eligibility: Applicants with high school education or above
Duration: 4 months
Description: Basic patient care, medical terminology, hygiene and safety protocols,
first aid certification, and elderly care specialization.
Outcome: Healthcare Assistant Certificate and placement in hospitals/clinics.
Recommended For: Female applicants, caregivers, those interested in healthcare.

PROGRAM 6: HOSPITALITY & TOURISM EXCELLENCE
Eligibility: Applicants with good communication skills
Duration: 2 months
Description: Customer service excellence, food safety, hotel operations,
tourism knowledge, and cultural awareness training.
Outcome: Hospitality Certificate and placement with hotel/restaurant partners.
Recommended For: Young applicants, those with customer service experience.

PROGRAM 7: LANGUAGE PROFICIENCY PROGRAM
Eligibility: All applicants
Duration: 3-6 months
Description: Arabic and English language courses at beginner, intermediate,
and advanced levels. Focus on workplace communication.
Outcome: Language proficiency certification.
Recommended For: Non-Arabic speakers, those with limited English.

PROGRAM 8: FINANCIAL LITERACY WORKSHOP
Eligibility: All support program recipients
Duration: 2 weeks (intensive)
Description: Personal budgeting, debt management, savings strategies,
understanding credit, and basic investment knowledge.
Outcome: Financial Management Certificate.
Recommended For: All applicants with debt-to-income issues.

PROGRAM 9: CAREER COUNSELING & JOB MATCHING
Eligibility: All unemployed applicants
Duration: Ongoing
Description: One-on-one career assessment, resume building, interview preparation,
skills gap analysis, and direct job matching with partner employers.
Outcome: Employment placement.
Recommended For: All unemployed or underemployed applicants.

PROGRAM 10: WOMEN EMPOWERMENT INITIATIVE
Eligibility: Female applicants, especially single mothers and widows
Duration: 4 months
Description: Comprehensive program combining skills training, financial literacy,
childcare support, and workplace integration. Includes tailoring, beauty services,
home-based business development.
Outcome: Multiple certifications and business/employment placement.
Recommended For: Female applicants with dependents, limited work experience.

RECOMMENDATION GUIDELINES:
- Unemployed with no skills: Programs 1, 7, 8, 9
- Unemployed with experience: Programs 2, 9
- Part-time seeking full-time: Programs 2, 6, 9
- Self-employed struggling: Programs 3, 8
- Young job seekers (18-25): Programs 1, 4, 5, 6, 7
- Career changers (35+): Programs 2, 4, 5
- Women with dependents: Programs 10, 1, 8
- High debt individuals: Programs 8, 9
